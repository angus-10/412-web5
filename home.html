About Us

Howard S. Billings High School Athletics Program offers a diverse array of sports, guided by experienced coaches, and bolstered by top-notch facilities. Our strong emphasis on sportsmanship and a balanced approach to academics and athletics distinguishes our program. Join our community to be a part of a dynamic and well-rounded athletic journey. Our History

Howard S. Billings High School, located in Châteauguay, Quebec, boasts a rich and storied history that spans over several decades. Established in 1967, the school has been a cornerstone of the community, providing generations of students with a nurturing environment for academic and personal growth. Named after Howard S. Billings, a prominent educator and public servant, the school has consistently upheld a commitment to excellence in education...
