HSB Sports Website Creation

Howard S. Billings is an English public high school in Chateauguay, Quebec. The main service they offer is secondary education from grades 7-11. I was hired to create a sports page for the high school athletic program to link off of their official website.

What needed to be acheieved?

Howard S. Billings had no official athletics landing page on its website, and navigating through different Facebook pages just wasn’t cutting it for a public school that was trying to establish itself as a top athletic school in the GMAA.

The process

I was hired to organize this problem and design a professional website dedicated to direct traffic to the various athletic clubs offered at Howard S. Billings High School. I had full creative control over the HTML and CSS of this project.

With a user-friendly interface, the website’s primary focus is easy navigation, allowing the user to effortlessly navigate to and from different sports clubs while staying on the same website.

The result

Multiple levels of navigation are on every page, simplifying the experience for the user.

Search bars are included on select pages to simplify the experience of finding a certain result.

Up-to-date, high-quality images bring the website to the next level by immersing the user into the sport with the athletes.
